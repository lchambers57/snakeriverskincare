Our Story

Snake River Skincare started with a simple idea: skin care should feel clean, confident, and uncomplicated. After years of trying products that were either too harsh or too fussy, Chris began creating small-batch formulas that focused on gentle, effective ingredients.

Inspired by the calm and strength of the Snake River, each product is designed to support daily routines without overwhelming the skin. Our line is grounded in minimal, thoughtful ingredients that work with your skin—not against it.

What We Value

  • Simplicity: Easy routines that are realistic and repeatable.
  • Gentleness: Formulas that respect sensitive and everyday skin.
  • Honesty: Clear, straightforward product descriptions and use.
  • Consistency: Products built to support long-term skin health.
  • Eco-friendly: All naturally formulated products.
